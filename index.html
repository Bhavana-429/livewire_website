Welcome to Livewire

Livewire is not just a dance club — it's the heartbeat of VNRVJIET’s cultural spirit. We are a family of passionate performers, dedicated to turning every rhythm into a story. Whether you groove to Hip-Hop or flow with Contemporary vibes, your stage awaits here. Every rehearsal, every beat, every spotlight moment — is a chance to express your soul. From winning competitions to setting the stage on fire during fests — we dance not just with our feet, but with our hearts. So if you're ready to move, shine, and grow with a team that believes in you — Livewire is the place to be. Let’s create magic, together.
